Solution to solve the issue of not getting the server list
Step 1: Download the jsonfiles file from repo.  (.rar or .zip it's the same)
Step 2: Overwrite your original files at \steam\steamapps\common\The Front\ProjectWar\ExtraFiles
Step 3: Restart the game
